Dr. Ebere Ibezim, PT, DPT

Head Physiotherapist & Clinical Director

Dr. Ibezim is a highly skilled physiotherapist with over 20 years of experience in treating musculoskeletal conditions, sports injuries, and post-operative rehabilitation. His patient-centered approach combines evidence-based treatment with compassionate care to help patients achieve their recovery goals.

With extensive training in manual therapy and sports rehabilitation, Dr. Ibezim has helped thousands of patients return to their active lifestyles and achieve optimal physical function.
